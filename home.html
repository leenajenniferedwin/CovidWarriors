ABOUT US

This website, called COVID WARRIORS, will provide verified information on the availability of oxygen cylinders and Remdesivir. The second wave of COVID-19 has hit the entire nation quite strongly. One of the consequences of the ongoing pandemic is the unavailability of verified information. While various information is being floated on the social media platforms but the information lacks authenticity.

A person gets to access the verified information on the status of availability of different supply items such as oxygen cylinders and Remdesivir according to users input such as location and quantity needed.
Hospitals support by registering and giving information of the stock they have of oxygen cylinders and Remdesivir.

Users must understand that the data provided on the website is collected and verified by volunteers to the best of their ability. However, users have been requested to reverify and confirm the identity of any vendor or service provider that they find through the website.
